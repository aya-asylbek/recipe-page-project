Grilled chicken kebobs with vegetables

A grilled chicken kebab recipe is a must-have for the summer grilling season! This recipe features juicy chicken in a sweet and savory marinade with crisp tender grilled vegetables for a crowd-pleasing meal!Kebabs are one of the best grilling options- they prep and cook quickly and easily, are versatile, and are irresistibly delicious. This chicken recipe is bursting with sweet flavor from the honey complimented by savory notes from the garlic and a hint of spice from the sriracha. The vegetables lighten the meal, which is guaranteed to be a favorite at your summer cookouts!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Honey
