Grilled Chicken Kabobs with Vegetables

A grilled chicken kebab recipe is a must-have for the summer grilling season! This recipe features juicy chicken in a sweet and savory marinade with crisp tender grilled vegetables for a crowd-pleasing meal! This chicken recipe is bursting with sweet flavor from the honey complimented by savory notes from the garlic and a hint of spice from the sriracha. The vegetables lighten the meal, which is guaranteed to be a favorite at your summer cookouts!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Honey
  • Mayonnaise
  • Oil
  • Mushrooms

Nutrition: 140 kcal | 11g Carbs | 2g Protein | 11g Fat

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the ingredients for the skewers...
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine mayonnaise, oil, hot sauce, minced garlic cloves, and honey.
  4. In a large bowl, combine chopped bell peppers, onion, mushrooms, and cubed chicken. Pour over the wet marinade ingredients and mix to coat everything evenly. Then, sprinkle over the dry seasonings and toss again. Cover with foil, and marinade in the fridge for 30 minutes to 4 hours, or overnight.
  5. Once the chicken has marinaded, thread the chicken and vegetables onto skewers...
  6. Grill the skewers over medium heat for 12-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked on all sides.
  7. Serve and enjoy, friends!
